### About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Dalian University of Technology, advised by Prof. Hongyu Wang. My research focuses on machine learning and pattern recognition for hyperspectral remote sensing, especially hyperspectral target detection, anomaly detection, image fusion, and intelligent interpretation.

Before my doctoral study, I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Jiangnan University and my B.S. degree in Digital Media Technology from Hubei Minzu University.

### Research Interests

- Hyperspectral target detection and anomaly detection
- Spectral-spatial representation learning
- Remote sensing image understanding and intelligent interpretation
- Deep unfolding, matrix decomposition, sparse representation, and state space models
- Cross-dataset hyperspectral image classification and domain adaptation
